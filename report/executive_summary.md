## Objective
The objective of this analysis is to evaluate sales performance across products, regions, and customers, and to identify actionable opportunities for revenue growth.

---

## Data Overview
The dataset contains transactional B2B sales data from pharmacies across multiple regions, including product categories, quantities sold, and revenue.

---

## Key Findings
- Sales activity is concentrated in a small number of repeat pharmacy accounts.
- Certain product categories contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
- Sales performance varies significantly by region and time period.

---

## Business Implications
- Strengthening relationships with high-performing accounts can increase revenue stability.
- Optimizing the product mix can improve overall sales efficiency.
- Regional performance differences suggest opportunities for targeted sales strategies.

---

## Next Steps
- Deeper customer-level analysis to identify high-value accounts.
- Trend analysis over longer time periods.
- Integration of forecasting techniques for sales planning.
