# Insights & Business Recommendations

## Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Concentration
Sales revenue is concentrated in a relatively small number of pharmacy accounts. Repeat customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue, indicating strong dependence on key accounts.

### 2. Product Performance
Supplement products generate the majority of revenue, while specific categories such as digestive and cardio health show strong but uneven performance across regions.

### 3. Regional Differences
Sales performance varies noticeably by region. Attica shows consistent volume, while other regions present opportunities for targeted growth initiatives.

### 4. Purchase Patterns
Certain products are repeatedly purchased together, suggesting potential for bundled offerings and cross-selling strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Focus on High-Value Accounts
Prioritize relationship management and tailored offers for top-performing pharmacies to strengthen retention and increase average order value.

### 2. Optimize Product Mix
Increase focus on high-performing product categories while reassessing underperforming items to improve overall sales efficiency.

### 3. Regional Sales Strategy
Develop region-specific sales strategies to address performance gaps and capitalize on local market dynamics.

### 4. Cross-Selling Opportunities
Introduce bundled product offerings based on recurring purchase patterns to encourage higher basket sizes.

---

## Strategic Value
Implementing these recommendations can support more stable revenue streams, improved customer relationships, and data-driven sales planning.
