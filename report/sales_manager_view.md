## What Is Working Well
- Strong repeat purchasing from key pharmacy accounts indicates stable relationships.
- Supplement products consistently drive the majority of revenue.
- Attica shows reliable sales volume and predictable performance.

---

## What Needs Attention
- Revenue is concentrated in a limited number of pharmacy accounts, increasing dependency risk.
- Performance varies significantly across regions, suggesting uneven market penetration.
- Certain product categories underperform compared to their potential.

---

## Key Opportunities
- Upsell and cross-sell high-performing supplement products to existing top accounts.
- Expand targeted sales efforts in regions outside Attica.
- Bundle frequently purchased products to increase average order value.

---

## Recommended Actions (Next 90 Days)
1. Prioritize top-performing pharmacies with tailored commercial offers.
2. Launch bundled product promotions focused on supplements.
3. Review regional sales coverage and adjust focus where growth potential exists.

- Average order value per pharmacy
- Revenue contribution by region
