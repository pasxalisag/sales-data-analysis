## Overview
This section summarizes the key sales performance indicators derived from the available transactional data.

---

## Core KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|-----|------------|
| Total Revenue | Total sales revenue generated across all regions and products |
| Total Units Sold | Total number of product units sold |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Average revenue per invoice |
| Revenue per Pharmacy | Average revenue generated per pharmacy account |
| Top Product Category | Product category contributing the highest share of revenue |
| Top Region | Region with the highest total revenue |

---

## KPI Interpretation
- Revenue-based KPIs provide a high-level view of overall sales performance.
- Account-level metrics highlight dependency on key pharmacy customers.
- Product and regional KPIs support targeted commercial decision-making.

---

## Business Use
These KPIs can be used for:
- Monthly sales performance reviews
- Account prioritization
- Product portfolio evaluation
- Strategic sales planning
